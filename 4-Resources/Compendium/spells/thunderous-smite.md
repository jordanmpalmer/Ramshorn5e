# Thunderous Smite
*1st-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

The first time you hit with a melee weapon attack during this spell's duration, your weapon rings with thunder that is audible within 300 feet of you, and the attack deals an extra `dice: 2d6|avg` (`2d6`) thunder damage to the target. Additionally, if the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away from you and knocked [prone](4-Resources/Compendium/rules/conditions.md#prone).

**Classes**: [Paladin](4-Resources/Compendium/classes/paladin.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 282*
