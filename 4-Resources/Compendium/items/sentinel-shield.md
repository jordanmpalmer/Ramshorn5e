# Sentinel Shield
*Shield, major, uncommon*  

- **Armor Class**: 2
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

While holding this shield, you have advantage on initiative rolls and Wisdom ([Perception](4-Resources/Compendium/rules/skills.md#Perception)) checks. The shield is emblazoned with a symbol of an eye.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 199*
