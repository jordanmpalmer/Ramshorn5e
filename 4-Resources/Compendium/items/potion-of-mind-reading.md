# Potion of Mind Reading
*Potion, minor, rare*  


When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [detect thoughts](4-Resources/Compendium/spells/detect-thoughts.md) spell (save DC 13). The potion's dense, purple liquid has an ovoid cloud of pink floating in it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 188. Available in the SRD.*
