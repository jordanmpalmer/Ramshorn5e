# Ring of Jumping
*Ring, major, uncommon (requires attunement)*  


While wearing this ring, you can cast the [jump](4-Resources/Compendium/spells/jump.md) spell from it as a bonus action at will, but can target only yourself when you do so.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 191. Available in the SRD.*
