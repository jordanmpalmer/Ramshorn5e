# Map or Scroll Case
*Adventuring Gear*  

- **Cost**: 1 gp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This cylindrical leather case can hold up to ten rolled-up [sheets of paper](4-Resources/Compendium/items/paper-one-sheet.md) or five rolled-up [sheets of parchment](4-Resources/Compendium/items/parchment-one-sheet.md).

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 151. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
