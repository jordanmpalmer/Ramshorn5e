# Boon of the Unfettered

You have advantage on ability checks made to resist being [grappled](4-Resources/Compendium/rules/conditions.md#grappled). In addition, you can use an action to automatically escape a grapple or free yourself of restraints of any kind. 

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 232*
