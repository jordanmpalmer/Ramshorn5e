# Boldrei
*Source: Eberron: Rising from the Last War p. 141, Tasha's Cauldron of Everything p. 32, Tasha's Cauldron of Everything p. 34*

- **Alignment**: Unaligned
- **Category**: The Sovereign Host
- **Domains**: Life, Peace, Twilight
- **Pantheon**: Eberron
- **Province**: Community, home
